A friend that keeps you on your toes.

Download for Android
A Tingo is a friend on my phone, slimier to a Tamagotchi, that understands what I have to say. This app allows me to track the steps I have taken in a day and use them to earn berries or XP, which are used to level up and feed my Tingo, motivating me to stay in shape and keep my friend healthy.

Information for Beta Testers

How to Install

While on the phone you would like to install TIngo on, click the green button at the top of this webpage that says 'Download for Android'. This will direct you to the page for Tingo on the Google Play Store. Hit the install button, wait for the download bar to finish and hit the open button to launch the app.

Using Features

Health

The health bar is the red bar on the top of the screen. Over time, it will start to decrease as the Tingo gets hungrier. When the health bar is low, press the feed berries button on the bottom right side of the page. If the Health gets too low, the Tingo will die.

Earning Berries

To earn berries to feed the Tingo, you will need to walk around with your phone. After a certain number of steps, you will be able to buy a berry. To do this, hit the menu button to go to the menu, then hit the buy berry button.

Death

If the Tingo does not eat any berries, over time its health will decrease. If the health bar goes all the way down, it will die and you will have to make a new Tingo.

Sounds and Animations

If you turn your volume up on your phone, you will be able to hear noises the Tingo makes. The Tingo can also react to seeing you, hearing what you say, or being fed by making a noise and performing an animation like jumping or doing a flip.

Moving Around the Room

To move around the room, press your finger in the middle of the screen and move it left and right.

Talking To the Tingo

To talk to the Tingo, turn the volume up on your phone and wait until you hear the tone. Once the tone plays, say what you would like to the Tingo and it will react based off of what you said.

Earning XP

The amount of XP that a Tingo has is related to the number of steps you have taken during the lifetime of the current Tingo. If you walk a lot and remember to feed your Tingo, It can get to a high level.
